About Us

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to clean water. Our mission is to make it happen, one community at a time.

Our Story

Charity Water was founded to help people around the world get access to safe, clean drinking water. We work with local partners and communities to build sustainable water projects.

Visible Impact : Gondwana Village

Gondwana residents lacked drinking water, relying on contaminated sources. After Charity Water's intervention, they now have access to clean water, transforming their health and daily lives. Mariama, a local mother, shares how this change has improved her family's well-being and future prospects.

Future Prospects

We aim to expand our clean water projects to more communities in need, ensuring a healthier future for all.
